Looking to improve student writing and research skills in your courses?
As you are preparing and getting excited for the 2017-2018 academic year,
WI+RE would love to offer our support and resources.

WI+RE (Writing Instruction + Research Education) is a UCLA community of students, instructors, and academic support staff focused on helping all learners succeed in their writing and research projects. We provide online access to a systematic foundation of research, writing, and information literacy skills. Our resources are designed to empower learners with practical skills and resources necessary to excel at UCLA and beyond.


WI+RE is dedicated to making our modules accessible and easy to integrate for any discipline or teaching style. We have modules on CCLE that can be easily assigned and graded, Youtube videos that can be widely shared, and a website that hosts all of our resources and allows students to browse for what they need.
